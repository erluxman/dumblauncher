# FocusLauncher — Vision & Philosophy

## What Is This?

A hyper-restrictive Android launcher that makes productivity the path of least resistance and distraction physically/socially/financially painful. Not a "screen time tracker." A behavioral architecture system.

## Core Principles

1. **Friction is the product** — Don't block. Make distraction progressively harder, uglier, slower, more embarrassing.
2. **Social > Willpower** — Individual discipline fails. Group accountability, peer pressure, and reputation systems succeed.
3. **Identity over rules** — Frame every action as a vote for who you're becoming. "Consumer" vs "Creator."
4. **Uninstall = group decision** — You can't quit alone. Your accountability group must approve your exit.
5. **Compound rewards** — Good behavior earns compound interest (time, money, features, social status).
6. **Honest mirror** — Show reality without judgment. Numbers, patterns, predictions. Let truth do the work.
7. **Boredom is a feature** — Intentionally leave dead space. Train tolerance for non-stimulation.

## Target User

- Knows they're addicted to phone
- Has tried (and failed) with screen time apps
- Willing to accept EXTREME measures
- Values long-term goals over short-term comfort
- Has at least 1-2 friends willing to join (group mechanics)

## What Makes This Different

| Existing Apps | FocusLauncher |
|---|---|
| Timer-based blocks | Psychological, social, financial, physical friction |
| Individual willpower | Group accountability + peer uninstall approval |
| Easy to bypass/uninstall | Device Admin + social locks + financial stakes |
| Shows stats after the fact | Intervenes IN the moment of weakness |
| One-size-fits-all | AI learns YOUR specific weakness patterns |

## Design Philosophy

- Minimalist home screen (calendar + current task + timer only)
- Apps are NOT visible by default — must be intentionally summoned
- Beautiful when disciplined, ugly when failing (earned aesthetics)
- No notifications from distraction apps — ever
- All social features are opt-in but strongly encouraged
